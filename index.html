About Me

Who am I? Hi, I'm Jenna Wu-Cardona; I'm 15 years old, and in 10th grade at Summit Shasta. I was born on April 17, 2001, in Oakland, and I lived there until I moved to South San Francisco, where I've lived since I was 8. I live with my parents, two younger sisters, and a lot of pets.

My Favorite Things Some things I like are Hamilton, GIlmore Girls, and a lot of books. Hamilton is a Broadway musical about the life of Alexander Hamilton. I like it because it's really interesting, has good, well written music, and I feel like I'm learning a lot, because it's very historically accurate. Gilmore Girls is a TV show about a single mother and daughter, who live in a small town and grow up over the course of the show. I got into it because of my mom, who was watching it, and told me to watch it, so then I loved it. Books have always been a large part of my life, and some of my favorite series are The Betsy-Tacy Series and Harry Potter.

Dance One of my hobbies is dance, which I used to do a lot. However, because I had surgery for my torn ACL and meniscus in May, I have just been recovering and will be this year. I really miss it, but hopefully will be able to return slowly in the winter/spring. My favorite type of dance is contemporary, but I did/will do many other types also.
